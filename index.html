About ANVESHANE

A celebration of innovation, talent, and the spirit of engineering at Bahubali College of Engineering, Shravanabelagola.

Where Ideas Come Alive

ANVESHANE is the flagship annual college fest of Bahubali College of Engineering (BCE), designed for BCE students only. It celebrates our community with internal technical challenges, cultural showcases, and student-led workshops.

From competitive coding to robotics, from dance battles to art exhibitions — ANVESHANE is a platform where our passion meets purpose. Join us and be part of something extraordinary.
