About Anweshane - 2K26

A celebration of innovation, talent, and the spirit of engineering at Bahubali College of Engineering, Shravanabelagola.

Where Ideas Come Alive

About Anweshane - 2K26 is the flagship annual college fest of Bahubali College of Engineering (BCE), designed for BCE students only. It celebrates our community with internal technical challenges, cultural showcases, and student-led workshops.

From competitive coding to robotics and from dance battles to art exhibitions, About Anweshane - 2K26 is a platform where passion meets purpose. Join us and be part of something extraordinary.
