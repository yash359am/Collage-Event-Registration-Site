About ANVESHANE

A celebration of innovation, talent, and the spirit of engineering at Bahubali College of Engineering, Shravanabelagola.

Where Ideas Come Alive

ANVESHANE is the flagship annual fest of Bahubali College of Engineering (BCE), Hassan District, Karnataka. It brings together the brightest minds from colleges across the state for an electrifying mix of technical challenges, cultural showcases, and innovative workshops.

From competitive coding to robotics, from dance battles to art exhibitions — ANVESHANE is a platform where passion meets purpose. Join us and be part of something extraordinary.
