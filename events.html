Installation Competition

Technical

Transform trash into treasure! Create a stunning art installation using waste and recycled materials. Unleash your creativity and spread the message of sustainability through art.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Each team must consist of exactly 4 members.
  • Teams must bring their own waste materials (plastic, cardboard, bottles, cans, old CDs, scrap, etc.).
  • No pre-made or store-bought decorative items allowed.
  • Installation must be completed within the allotted time.
  • Judging criteria: creativity, theme, use of waste material, and presentation.
  • The installation must convey a meaningful message (environment, social, etc.).
  • Participants must carry their college ID cards.
  • The organizer's decision is final and binding.
